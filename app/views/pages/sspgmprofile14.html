Profile of Hirajibhai Punjabhai Jagatiya

Mr.Hirajibhai Punjabhai Jagatiya is the son of Mr. Punjabhai Jagatiya. He born in Jam Raval a village located in Jamnagar District, Gujarat(lndia) in Sept. 1960. He became graduate in Bcom from Shri K. H. Madhwani Commerce College, Porbandar. Since his graduation he was very much interested in literacy work and gradually he involved in his community as well as political activity. Here is brief overview of his life up to now.
